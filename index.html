A Fun And Easy Way To Learn About Money And SAVING.

Pine Finance is a mobile app designed to educate children on the credit system with money got by completing chores.

Featured

Income and credit made simple.
Children learn how to monitor their credit effectively by tracking their income with every task completed, set savings goals, spend wisely, and invest. Parents set flexible controls and get real-time notifications every time their kids complete tasks assigned to them.
